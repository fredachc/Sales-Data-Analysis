#Sales Data Analysis Project#

/-Objective-/
This project aims to analyze sales performance using SQL and Excel to identify key business insights across product categories, regions, and discount strategies.

/-Tools Used-/
- Excel (Pivot Tables, Dashboard)
- PostgreSQL (SQL Queries)
- Data Visualization

/-Key Analysis-/
1. Sales Performance
- Calculated total sales and profit
- Derived overall profit margin
2. Category Analysis
- Identified high-performing product categories
- Compared revenue vs profitability
3. Regional Analysis
- Analyzed sales and profit by region
- Found performance differences across markets
4. Discount Impact
- Evaluated relationship between discount and profit
- Identified potential over-discounting issues

/-Key Insights-/
- Technology category generates higher profit margins
- Some regions have high sales but lower profitability
- Higher discount levels are associated with lower profit margins

/-Recommendations-/
- Optimize discount strategies to improve profitability
- Focus on high-margin product categories
- Review regional pricing and cost structures
